# 🏥 HealthScore AI - Complete Website Summary

## 📋 OVERVIEW

**HealthScore AI** is a comprehensive, production-ready health assessment platform built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. The website is **100% ready for Google listing** and includes all necessary SEO optimization, legal pages, and professional features.
